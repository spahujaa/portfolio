Story

I'm a UX Consultant at Deloitte US Offices of India — currently leading enterprise-scale AI tools and $6.7M+ client pursuits. With 5+ years spanning healthcare, fintech, and automotive, I work best at the intersection of ambiguous business problems and complex user needs, translating stakeholder expectations into experiences that drive measurable outcomes.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm chasing light with a camera — hunting for the perfect iced latte in cities I probably booked a flight to on a whim.

"If one stays true to the process, the result always follows."

Being observant and proactive are two key aspects of my work ethic. I try to find ways to make anything better. My interests also include striking conversations with strangers and capturing compelling frames.

I hold a B.Des in Humanising Technology from the School of Design, NMIMS Mumbai (3.61/4 CGPA), and hold a research certification from the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.
